DEPARTMENTS

Below are the departments of different services offered at our hospital.

INPATIENT

We offer inpatient services for critically ill patients which is manned by a group of specialised doctors. We offer quality services and quality care to our patients.

OUTPATIENT

Our outpatient offer a wide range services from accidents and emergency,wellnes clinic, family planning and physiotherapy to mention but a few.

REPRODUCTIVE HEALTH

We believe that every women should be able to access Antenatal care no matter their case. We have a group of experts who attend to our patients.

PAEDIATRIC AND CHILD HEALTH

We offer both child baby friendly and KEPI vaccines by our qualified nurses. Routine child health assesment and nutritional counselling and support for under five years.

CONSULTATION CLINIC

The following services are availabe at our consultation clinic;Gynaecology, paediatric clinic,Orthopaedic clinic,pain management,TB clinic to mention but a few.

DENTAL SERVICES

we offer a wide range of dental services to adults and children:Aesthetic dentistry, Veneers, Tooth coloured white fillings, Teeth whitening, Root canal treatment, Implantology.
